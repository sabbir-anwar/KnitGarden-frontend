About Our Company

Welcome to Knit Garden, A total solution for knit garments.

Knit Garden, is a 100% export oriented composite knit garments factory. Mainly oriented towards manufacturing & exporting knit garments product. Establishment at 1992. Its Number of Employee is 800.

Factory Floor Space is 30000sft.
